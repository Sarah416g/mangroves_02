We Need Mangroves

Mangroves are some of the only trees that not only survive but thrive in salty water. They promote biodiversity becuase so many species of animals, and plants depend on mangroves, including humans. They protect agaisnt extreme weather and natural disasters, mangroves absorb the impact of waves reducing harm on vulnrable coastal communities. They also provide food security for these coastline communities. In addition they are amazing for storing carbon from the atmosphere and with the increase of greenhouse gases they serve an important role.
